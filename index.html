What is a conversion — and why does it matter?

A conversion is the moment a visitor does what you want them to do. It's the action that turns a stranger on Google into a real lead for your business. Without tracking conversions, we'd only know how many people clicked the ad — not how many actually contacted you.

Think of it this way: clicks are visitors walking into your shop. Conversions are the ones who actually ask to speak to someone.

What counts as a conversion for your campaign
Counts as a conversion
Filling in your contact form
Clicking to call from the ad
Clicking a "get a quote" button
Does not count
Just viewing the ad
Clicking and leaving immediately
Visiting without taking action

We configure conversion tracking before we spend a single rand. Every enquiry gets recorded so we always know which keywords and ads are generating real leads — not just traffic.
